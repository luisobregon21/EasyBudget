How it all fits together
💳 Credit Card flow
Log expense when it happens (e.g. DoorDash $20 → Food/Wants). At month end, the credit card bill appears as a Bills line item — the app already counted those charges, so it's just a payment, not a new expense.
💵 Cash + Multi-currency
Log cash when you spend it. For foreign currency, enter the local amount — the app converts to USD at today's rate and stores both. Your trip envelope tracks cordobas/quetzales; your monthly budget sees USD.
